Place your sprite sheet image in this folder.

Recommended file name: cat-sprite-sheet.png

Expected layout:

- 6 rows
- 4 columns
- equal-size frames
- top row = walk down
- row 2 = walk right
- row 3 = walk up
- row 4 = walk left
- row 5 = idle
- row 6 = groom

The app slices the sheet dynamically based on the total image size and the 6x4 grid.
